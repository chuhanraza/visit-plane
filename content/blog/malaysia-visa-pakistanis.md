- **Employment Pass:** For skilled professionals

## ✅ Who Needs This Visa?

Great news for Pakistani travelers: if you're planning tourism for up to 90 days, you don't need a visa! Pakistan is one of 183 countries with visa-free entry to Malaysia. However, if you plan to stay longer than 90 days, work, study, or engage in business activities, you'll need the appropriate visa category. Extensions and other visa types require formal applications.

**You need a formal visa if:**
- Planning to stay beyond 90 days
- Intending to work or conduct business
- Enrolling in educational programs
- Seeking student or employment passes
- Planning multiple entries with extended stays

## 📝 Application Process

Pakistani passport holders can typically enter visa-free and extend within Malaysia. However, if applying abroad, follow these steps:

1. **Assess Your Visa Need** — Confirm whether you need a visa (most tourists don't for 90-day stays)

2. **Visit Malaysian Diplomatic Mission** — Go to the Malaysian High Commission or Consulate in Pakistan

3. **Obtain Application Form** — Collect the appropriate application form for your visa category

4. **Prepare Documentation** — Gather all required documents in organized folder

5. **Submit Application** — Present application and documents during consulate hours

6. **Attend Interview** — Brief questions about travel purpose and duration (if required)

7. **Pay Visa Fee** — Fee varies by visa category (approximately PKR 3,000-5,000)

8. **Receive Processing Confirmation** — Get date for passport collection

9. **Collect Visa** — Retrieve your passport with the visa stamp

10. **Travel to Malaysia** — You're ready for your Malaysian adventure!

## 💡 Tips for Approval

Pakistani applicants have excellent success rates for Malaysian visas. Strengthen your application with these strategies:

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Do Pakistani citizens need a visa for Malaysia?**
A: Not for tourism! Pakistani passport holders get 90 days visa-free. Apply for visa only if staying longer or for work/education.

**Q: How long can I stay in Malaysia?**
A: Initial visit: 90 days visa-free. Extensions: additional 30 days possible (total 120 days). Longer stays require appropriate visa category.

**Q: Can I extend my stay in Malaysia?**
A: Yes! Visit any Immigration Department office in Malaysia (PUTRAJAYA, KL, Penang, etc.) to apply for 30-day extension.

**Q: Can I work with a tourist visa?**
A: No. Working requires an Employment Pass. Tourist visas explicitly prohibit employment.

**Q: What if my passport expires soon?**
A: Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your return date. Renew your Pakistani passport before traveling if needed.
