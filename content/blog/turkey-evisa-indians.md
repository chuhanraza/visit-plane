## 🌍 Overview

The Turkish e-Visa is an electronic authorization that allows Indian nationals to enter and stay in Turkey for up to 90 days within a 180-day period. Launched in 2013, Turkey's e-Visa system revolutionized travel documentation — no more embassy visits, no waiting in lines, no complicated paperwork. For Indian citizens, applying for a Turkish e-Visa is faster and simpler than almost any other visa application process globally. The 2026 system is even more streamlined, with 99.8% approval rate for valid applications and approval typically granted within 1-2 hours.

**Turkey e-Visa at a Glance:**
- **Processing:** 100% online application
- **Approval Time:** 1-24 hours (typically within 1 hour)
- **Validity:** 180 days from issue date
- **Stay Duration:** 90 days total (renewable)
- **Cost:** $60 USD (approximately ₹5,000 INR)
- **2026 Approval Rate:** 99.8% for Indian applicants

## ✅ Who Needs This Visa?

All Indian nationals require a Turkey e-Visa for entry, unless they're Turkish citizens or hold a Turkish residence permit. The e-Visa is suitable for tourists, businesspeople, students, and visitors. It covers multiple purposes including leisure travel, business meetings, educational tours, and visiting family. Even if you have a Turkish residence permit or employment contract, you still need an initial e-Visa for entry (which can then be converted to other visa categories in-country).

**You need a Turkey e-Visa if:**
- Planning tourism or leisure travel to Turkey
- Attending business meetings or conferences
- Visiting family or friends
- Participating in educational programs
- Any other temporary visit purposes

## 📝 Application Process

Applying for a Turkey e-Visa takes just 5-10 minutes. Follow these simple steps:

1. **Visit Official Website** — Go to www.evisa.gov.tr (the only official Turkish e-Visa site)

2. **Select "Apply"** — Click the application button on the homepage

3. **Choose India as Nationality** — Select "India" from the dropdown menu of nationalities

4. **Enter Passport Details** — Input your passport number and basic information exactly as it appears in your passport

5. **Fill Personal Information** — Complete your full name, date of birth, email address, and contact details

6. **Select Purpose & Duration** — Choose "Tourism" as purpose and select your intended stay length

7. **Review Information** — Carefully check all entered details for accuracy

8. **Read Declaration** — Review terms and conditions, then check the declaration box

9. **Proceed to Payment** — Click next to advance to payment gateway

10. **Pay Online** — Use debit/credit card to pay $60 USD fee

11. **Receive e-Visa** — Within 1-24 hours (usually within 1 hour), receive e-Visa via email

12. **Print and Carry** — Print the e-Visa and carry it along with your passport

## 💡 Tips for Approval

Turkish e-Visas have exceptional approval rates for Indian applicants. Ensure success with these simple tips:

**1. Use Official Website Only** — Always use www.evisa.gov.tr. Fake sites exist; using them wastes money and creates problems.

**2. Passport Accuracy** — Enter your passport number and details EXACTLY as they appear in your passport. Even tiny mistakes can cause issues.

**3. Valid Email Address** — Ensure your email is active and monitored. You'll receive the e-Visa link there within hours.

**4. Check Spelling** — Double-check spelling of your full name, especially if it has multiple parts or special characters.

**5. Appropriate Card** — Use a debit/credit card in good standing with no foreign transaction blocks.

**6. Print Your e-Visa** — Once approved, immediately print your e-Visa document. Carry it with your passport.

**7. Entry Compliance** — Turkish e-Visas are valid for entry 180 days from issue. Plan your travel accordingly.

**8. Backup Copy** — Save a digital copy of your e-Visa on your phone and email. Useful if physical copy gets lost.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is the official website www.evisa.gov.tr?**
A: Yes, absolutely. This is the ONLY official Turkish e-Visa website. Avoid unofficial sites which are scams.

**Q: How quickly will I receive my e-Visa?**
A: Most Indian applicants receive approval within 1 hour. Worst case, 24 hours. You receive it via email immediately.

**Q: Can I extend my Turkish e-Visa?**
A: No, but you can leave and re-enter Turkey during the 180-day validity period, restarting your 90-day stay.

**Q: What if my e-Visa application is rejected?**
A: Rejections are extremely rare (0.2%). If rejected, you can reapply immediately. Most rejections result from incorrect passport data.

**Q: Can I use an e-Visa to work in Turkey?**
A: No. e-Visas are for tourism only. Working requires an employment visa (obtained through Turkish employer).
