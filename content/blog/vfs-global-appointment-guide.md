## What Is VFS Global?

VFS Global is a private company contracted by governments to handle the front-end processing of visa applications. They are not part of the embassy — they do not make visa decisions. Their role is to:

- Accept and verify visa application documents
- Collect biometric data (fingerprints and photograph)
- Forward applications to the relevant embassy or immigration authority
- Return passports with visa decisions to applicants

**Countries using VFS Global (partial list):**
- All major Schengen countries: Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Austria, Belgium, Switzerland, and more
- United Kingdom (UKVI)
- Canada
- Australia
- South Africa
- Several others depending on your country of application

For Pakistani applicants, VFS Global operates application centres in Islamabad, Karachi, and Lahore for most major visa categories.

---

## Step 1: Create a VFS Global Account

1. Go to **vfsglobal.com** and select your **country of residence** (Pakistan) and the **destination country** you are applying for
2. Click "Book an Appointment" on the relevant country page
3. Create an account using your email address — use one you check regularly, as all communications go here
4. Verify your email address

**Tip:** Create your VFS account well before you are ready to book. The account creation is separate from appointment booking, and having it ready means you can pounce when slots open.

---

## Step 2: Select the Right Service

When logged in, you will be prompted to select:

1. **Country of Application** (Pakistan — Islamabad / Karachi / Lahore)
2. **Visa Category** — Tourist, Business, Family, Student, Work, etc.
3. **Number of Applicants** — book the correct number; family groups are often booked together

**Important:** Select the service that matches your application type. A mismatch between your booked service and your actual visa category can result in rejection at the counter.

---

## Step 3: Choose Your Appointment Slot

This is where many applicants struggle during peak season. Slots for popular embassies (Germany, UK, France) can be scarce in May–August.

1. Select your **preferred VFS centre** (Islamabad, Karachi, or Lahore)
2. Browse available dates — a calendar will show available slots in green
3. Select a date and time that works
4. Confirm the appointment — you will receive a confirmation email

**If no slots are available:**
- Check at different times of day — new slots are released dynamically, often in early morning hours (5–7 AM local time)
- Check different centres — a centre in a different city may have availability
- Set up browser refresh alerts or check every 24 hours; cancellations free up slots regularly

## Step 4: Pay VFS Fees

VFS charges a service fee on top of the embassy's visa fee. For Pakistan:

| Service | Approximate Cost (2026) |
|---|---|
| Standard Service Fee | PKR 8,000–12,000 per applicant |
| Premium Lounge Service | PKR 20,000–25,000 per applicant |
| Courier Service (passport return) | PKR 1,500–2,500 |
| Photo Service | PKR 500–800 |
| Document Scanning | PKR 500–1,000 |

VFS fees are typically paid at the centre on the day of your appointment, or via the portal during booking (varies by centre). Check the specific country's VFS page for the latest fee structure.

## Step 5: Prepare Your Documents

Before your appointment, organise your documents in the exact order specified in the embassy's official checklist. VFS officers check off documents against this list — a disorganised packet slows processing and risks items being missed.

**General document checklist:**
- Completed application form (printed)
- Passport (original + photocopy of all pages)
- Photographs (meeting specifications)
- Travel insurance certificate
- Flight reservation
- Hotel bookings
- Bank statements (3–6 months, bank-stamped)
- Employment letter and payslips
- Tax returns
- Cover letter
- VFS appointment confirmation
- Visa fee payment confirmation (if pre-paid)

## What Happens on the Day at VFS

1. **Arrival:** Arrive 15 minutes early. Bring your appointment confirmation. VFS centres are strict about entry — only the applicants listed in the booking are allowed inside.

2. **Document Verification:** A VFS officer checks your documents against the required list. Missing documents may be flagged here.

3. **Biometrics:** You will provide fingerprints (all 10 fingers) and a digital photograph. This takes 5–10 minutes. Biometric data is valid for 59 months for most Schengen countries once enrolled.

4. **Application Submission:** Your documents are collected and packaged for dispatch to the embassy.

5. **Fee Payment:** If not pre-paid, you pay VFS service fees and the visa fee at this stage.

6. **Receipt:** You receive a tracking receipt with a reference number. Use this to track your application online.

**Total time at VFS:** 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on the centre's queue on the day.

## Tips to Get Appointments Faster

1. **Check at off-peak hours** — new slots frequently appear in early morning (5–7 AM) when the system refreshes
2. **Check all three centres** (Islamabad, Karachi, Lahore) — one may have availability when others are full
3. **Monitor cancellations** — people cancel appointments regularly; availability can open same-day
4. **Use an appointment alert service** — several informal services (Telegram groups, WhatsApp channels) alert members when VFS slots become available
5. **Apply outside peak season** — if your travel is flexible, October–March has far more appointment availability than May–September

## VFS Premium Lounge

VFS offers a premium lounge service at select centres (Islamabad, Karachi). For a higher service fee (PKR 20,000–25,000), you get:

- Priority queue — skip the regular waiting area
- Private document submission with a dedicated officer
- Complimentary refreshments
- Often faster document dispatch to the embassy

This is worth considering for busy professionals who cannot afford to wait 2+ hours, or for large family applications where managing a queue is difficult.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I reschedule my VFS appointment?
Yes. Log in to your VFS account and select "Manage Appointment." You can reschedule up to 24–48 hours before your slot. Note that rescheduling during peak season may result in a significantly later date.

### Does VFS make visa decisions?
No. VFS only collects documents and biometrics. All visa decisions are made by the embassy or immigration authority of the destination country.

### What if I miss my VFS appointment?
You must rebook. Missed appointments do not receive refunds of service fees if pre-paid. Rebook as soon as possible — slots fill quickly during peak season.

### Can I bring someone with me to VFS?
Generally, only the applicants listed in the appointment can enter the processing area. Some centres allow one accompanying adult for child applicants or elderly applicants needing assistance.

### Is the VFS service fee refundable if my visa is rejected?
No. VFS service fees are non-refundable regardless of outcome. The visa fee itself is also non-refundable once the embassy processes the application.
