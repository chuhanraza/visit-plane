## Overview

Canada has emerged as a top travel destination for Pakistani citizens, offering diverse landscapes, vibrant cities, and exceptional hospitality. From the breathtaking Niagara Falls to the majestic Rocky Mountains, Canada provides unforgettable travel experiences. Obtaining a Canadian Tourist Visa (Temporary Resident Visa) as a Pakistani national requires careful documentation and planning. This guide provides comprehensive information about eligibility criteria, required documents, application procedures, processing timelines, and costs for Pakistani travelers planning a Canadian vacation in 2026.

## Eligibility Criteria

Pakistani applicants for a Canadian tourist visa must meet these requirements:

- **Valid Passport:** Passport must be valid for the entire duration of your intended stay
- **Purpose of Visit:** Clear and legitimate tourist purpose (sightseeing, family visit, business meetings)
- **Sufficient Funds:** Demonstrate financial capability to support your trip
- **Strong Ties to Pakistan:** Evidence of permanent residence and intention to return
- **Clean Background:** No criminal history or previous visa violations
- **No Deportation:** Must not have been deported from any country
- **Health Requirements:** Must not pose health risks; medical examination may be required
- **Return Intention:** Clear proof of ties to home country (property, employment, family)
- **Employment Status:** Generally employed or have stable income source

## Documentation Requirements

### Core Documents

- Completed application form (IMM 1419)
- Valid Pakistani passport
- Passport-sized photographs (2 recent, 35x45mm)
- Birth certificate or national ID copy
- Marriage certificate (if applicable)

### Financial Documentation

- Bank account statements (6-12 months)
- Proof of employment (employment letter, salary slips)
- Business registration documents (if self-employed)
- Property ownership documents
- Tax returns or income tax documents

### Travel Documents

- Flight booking confirmation
- Hotel reservation or accommodation proof
- Travel itinerary for the entire stay
- Return flight booking
- Travel insurance policy

### Supporting Documents

- Family composition documentation
- Proof of ties to Pakistan (property deeds, vehicle ownership)
- Employment contract or resignation letter
- University enrollment (if student)
- Sponsorship letter from Canadian friend/family (if applicable)

## Processing Timeline

Canadian visa applications for Pakistani citizens typically follow this timeline:

- **Application Submission:** At the nearest Canadian embassy or visa application center
- **Initial Document Review:** 2-3 working days
- **Processing Period:** 4-6 weeks (standard)
- **Biometric Appointment:** Scheduled within 1-2 weeks of submission
- **Final Decision:** Additional 2-4 weeks after biometrics
- **Visa Delivery:** Via courier service, 2-3 days
- **Total Time:** 6-10 weeks recommended

Processing times vary by season; applications submitted during peak season (March-August) may take longer. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before your intended travel date.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long can I stay in Canada with a tourist visa?**
A: Typically 6 months, though the border officer determines the exact duration upon entry.

**Q: Can I work while on a visitor visa?**
A: No, visitor visas do not permit employment of any kind.

**Q: Can I extend my stay in Canada?**
A: Yes, extensions are possible; apply before your current authorization expires.

**Q: Do I need a medical examination?**
A: Generally not required unless you've previously lived in high-risk countries for tuberculosis.

**Q: What if my visa is refused?**
A: You receive a refusal letter explaining reasons. You can reapply with additional supporting documents after addressing concerns.

**Q: Can family members apply together?**
A: Yes, spouses and dependent children can apply in the same batch, though each needs separate visa approval.

## Eligibility for Canada Tourist Visa

Pakistani nationals must meet specific criteria to qualify for a Canada Tourist Visa. You must be a Pakistani passport holder with a valid passport that extends at least 6 months beyond your stay in Canada. You should have a genuine reason for your visit — tourism, visiting family, attending events, or business purposes.

## Requirements for Canada Tourist Visa

**Personal Documents:**
- Valid Pakistani passport (minimum 6 months validity)
- Completed application form (IMM 0008)
- Supplementary family information form
- Passport photos (35×45 mm)
- Copy of CNIC

**Financial Documents:**
- Bank statements for 6 months

## Processing Timeline
