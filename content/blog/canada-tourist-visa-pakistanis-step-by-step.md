## Overview

Canada is a dream destination for Pakistani travelers, known for its natural beauty, welcoming culture, and diverse opportunities. Whether you're planning to visit Niagara Falls, explore the Rocky Mountains, or experience Canadian hospitality, obtaining a Canada Tourist Visa (Temporary Resident Visa) is your gateway. This step-by-step guide provides Pakistani citizens with complete information about Canada Tourist Visa requirements, application procedures, processing times, and costs.

## Eligibility for Canada Tourist Visa

Pakistani nationals must meet specific criteria to qualify for a Canada Tourist Visa. You must be a Pakistani passport holder with a valid passport that extends at least 6 months beyond your stay in Canada. You should have a genuine reason for your visit — tourism, visiting family, attending events, or business purposes.

To qualify, you must prove that you have sufficient financial resources to support yourself during your stay and can return to Pakistan. Additionally, you should have strong ties to Pakistan, such as property ownership, employment, or family, to demonstrate your intention to return home.

## Requirements for Canada Tourist Visa

**Personal Documents:**
- Valid Pakistani passport (minimum 6 months validity)
- Completed application form (IMM 0008)
- Supplementary family information form
- Passport photos (35×45 mm)
- Copy of CNIC

**Financial Documents:**
- Bank statements for 6 months
- Employment letter on company letterhead
- Pay stubs or salary slips
- Tax returns (last 2 years)
- Business registration (if self-employed)

**Travel Documents:**
- Hotel bookings or accommodation proof
- Flight itineraries (round-trip)
- Travel itinerary detailing your plans
- Return ticket or confirmation

**Supporting Documents:**
- Invitation letter (if visiting family/friends)
- Family documents (marriage certificate, birth certificates)
- Property ownership documents
- Business licenses or professional credentials

## Processing Timeline

Canada Tourist Visas for Pakistani applicants typically take 4–8 weeks to process. Processing times may extend during peak seasons (spring/summer) to 12–16 weeks. Apply at least 2–3 months before your intended travel date.

## Cost of Canada Tourist Visa

The Canada Tourist Visa application fee is CAD 100. If you need biometrics, there's an additional fee of CAD 85. The total cost is approximately CAD 185. If you choose to use a visa application center in Pakistan, there may be additional service charges.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long can I stay in Canada on a tourist visa?**
A: Canada typically grants stays of 6 months.

**Q: Can I work in Canada on a tourist visa?**
A: No, tourist visas do not permit employment in Canada.

**Q: Is travel insurance mandatory?**
A: While not mandatory, travel insurance is highly recommended for Canadian visits.

**Q: Can I extend my stay in Canada?**
A: Yes, you can apply for an extension at the IRCC office in Canada.

**Q: What if my visa application is rejected?**
A: You can reapply with additional supporting documents addressing the reasons for rejection.
